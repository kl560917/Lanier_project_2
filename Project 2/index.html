BEST FOOD ON CAMPUS

Introduction

My name is Kevin Lanier, I am a senior at Ohio University, and I love food. I have spent my last four years on campus at Ohio University and have had the chance to eat at all kinds of restaurants around the area. Here are my picks for the best restaurants on campus at Ohio University.

BAGEL STREET DELI

Best Bagels

Rank #1

Coming in at number one is Bagel Street Deli. This is my favorite spot to eat in Athens because the food is always great, you have a great view of the downtown Athens area, and the experience you get when you go to Bagel Street. If you go to Ohio University or you are just visiting regardless, this is a must stop.
